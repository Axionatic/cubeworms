## Cubeworms

Glowing creatures drifting in the void. They keep to themselves until you lure them out.

Built with [Processing 3](https://processing.org/).

### How to

Open the sketch folder in Processing and hit Run.

Left click to place a beacon. Left click again to detonate it. The worms will react.

Right-click and drag to look around. Scroll to zoom. Backspace to reset.

### Knobs and dials

Mess with `settings.pde`.
